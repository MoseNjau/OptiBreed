Monitoring Conditions For Storage of
BREEDING MATERIALS

The project aims to develop a web-based system for monitoring and tracking the storage conditions of breeding materials used in agricultural programs.

About Us

Monitoring Breeding Materials Initiative is a student-led initiative from JKUAT under the Facilitation of JHUB, focused on providing solutions for the monitoring and preservation of breeding programs across various species since 2024.


Breeding material management is critical for ensuring the quality, viability, and genetic integrity of breeding programs. Our emphasis lies in providing effective solutions for monitoring storage conditions, such as temperature, humidity, and light exposure, to safeguard the health and productivity of breeding materials.


Our aim is to ensure the maintenance of viable seeds and genetic materials in plants and animals by raising awareness about the importance of monitoring breeding material storage conditions. Through the promotion of best practices in storage management and the provision of educational resources, we empower breeders and researchers to optimize breeding outcomes and contribute to genetic diversity conservation efforts, preserving the legacy of biodiversity for future generations.

What we Do

Breeding Material Monitoring

This involves deploying sensors and data loggers in storage facilities to track key environmental factors such as temperature, humidity, and light exposure. By continuously monitoring these conditions, we ensure the quality, viability, and genetic integrity of the breeding materials, thereby contributing to the success of breeding programs.

Storage Condition Analysis

Our team analyzes the data collected from the monitoring system to identify trends, patterns, and anomalies in storage conditions. Through data analysis techniques, we gain insights into the factors influencing the health and productivity of breeding materials. By understanding the relationships between environmental variables and breeding outcomes, we can optimize storage practices and improve breeding program efficiency.

Awareness Campaigns

Through various outreach activities such as workshops, seminars, and social media campaigns, we aim to raise awareness about the significance of proper storage management in preserving genetic diversity and ensuring sustainable breeding practices.

Educational Resources

We develop and disseminate educational resources to empower breeders and researchers with the knowledge and tools needed to effectively monitor and manage storage conditions for breeding materials. These resources may include online tutorials, guides, and training materials covering topics such as sensor deployment, data analysis, and best practices in storage management.

Specialised Solutions

1. Customized Sensors: We will develop customized sensors tailored to the specific needs of breeding materials for different animal species. These sensors will be designed to accurately measure key parameters such as temperature, humidity, and light exposure, ensuring precise monitoring and control.

2. Wireless Connectivity: Our sensors will feature wireless connectivity capabilities, allowing seamless integration with a centralized monitoring platform. This enables real-time data collection and remote access to storage conditions, enhancing convenience and accessibility for farmers.

3. Smart Monitoring Platform: We will develop a user-friendly monitoring platform equipped with advanced analytics and visualization tools. Farmers can easily track storage conditions, set custom thresholds, and receive timely alerts or recommendations for corrective actions.

4. Scalability and Flexibility: Our solution will be scalable and adaptable to various farming environments and breeding programs. Whether it's monitoring eggs in an incubator, storing semen samples in a refrigeration unit, or housing livestock in barns, our sensors and platform can accommodate diverse needs.
